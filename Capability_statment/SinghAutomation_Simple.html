ABOUT US

Company Overview

Singh Automation, LLC delivers turnkey industrial automation and AI-powered systems for manufacturing, logistics, and mission-critical infrastructure. We engineer, integrate, and commission advanced robotic cells, intelligent material-handling systems, and high-performance computing environments that modernize operations for government and commercial clients.

From initial design through installation and ongoing support, we provide end-to-end solutions that bridge physical automation and digital intelligence. Based in Kalamazoo, MI with a sales branch in Irvine, CA, we're authorized system integrators for FANUC and Universal Robots.
